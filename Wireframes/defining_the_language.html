GRAPHIC LANGUAGE

IHTSDO

DEFINING THE LANGUAGE

Demonstrated within a range of modules, the graphic language has to work within the context of IHTSDO generated platforms and the wider digital environment / 3rd party platforms.

THE CHALLENGE

Provide subtle visual cues as to the heritage of the modules, whilst remaining ostensibly white label in appearance.

APPROACH

A minimal level of branding affords greater customisation within 3rd party platforms.

CONSIDERATIONS

 

Derived from the latest brand communications: Primary brand blue, rounded-serifed style of the Museo typeface and the use of white space supported by subtle key lines.
