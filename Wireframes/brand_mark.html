BRAND MARK

IHTSDO

INTERACTION

The brand mark identifies IHTSDO and when used as part of the online services, provides a reassurance and consistent experience.

WHAT IS IT FOR?

A widely accepted convention, on-click of the brand mark will link to the home page (of the current service).

BEHAVIOUR

The brand mark is an important element used as a part of the Masthead and Footer. Once the height of these have been determined, the brand mark size is dictated by a 'clear zone' surrounding it.
