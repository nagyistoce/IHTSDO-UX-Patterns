Positive Action

 

 

Modal windows allow for the presentation of additional information that has very limited space in the page layout. In modal windows, it is possible to present larger images, extra content, agreements, video or alerts/tips that the user will easily notice.

WHAT ARE THEY FOR?

MODALS

IHTSDO

INTERACTION

It is important and should be clear that a modal window has been invoked. This is easily achieved by fading out the page behind the window. This focuses the user's attention to the modal and away from the content behind.

BEHAVIOUR

A modal should be used when it is necessary to interrupt a user to inform them of legal or license agreements before they can proceed or for displaying related page content (images, additional copy etc.) without linking to another page.

HOW AND WHEN TO USE

ACTION BAR
