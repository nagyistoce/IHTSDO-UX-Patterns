INTERACTION

Links feature to lead users elsewhere within a service. They can be used as primary, stand-alone navigation or as simple links to further content.

WHAT ARE THEY FOR?

Abiding by a standard behavioural convention, hyperlinks act on-click, linking to additional content. They have both a default and rollover state that should be observed globally.

BEHAVIOUR

Hyperlinks are identified by their colour, which helps to differentiate them from headers and standard body copy. They have both a default and rollover state that should be observed consistently.

HOW AND WHEN TO USE

TOOLKIT

Try to avoid lengthy URLs, phrases or sentences as links. Also, be mindful to the size of links. They are more suited to smaller font sizer. Consider an alternative linking solution should a situation arise when a large scale link might be required. e.g. buttons or modules.
