BEHAVIOUR

A sidebar is a module comprised of smaller nested modules or individual UI elements such as headers, body text, links, radios, checks and icons. The smaller nested modules are specifically designed for the location of their use. Abiding by the defined grid structures and observing moderate text lengths, sidebars should be easily constructed in a modular fashion.

 

They should be used on a page where necessary as support.
