The fundamental part of any balanced, ordered and concise layout is the grid. It is the foundations of a page structure on-which all content sits. This ensures a tidy and consistent experience globally.

 

The IHTSDO service UIs are based upon a 16-column grid. This provides a flexible foundation on-which to accommodate the flowing content and multiple layouts required to most effectively convey all of the information.

BEHAVIOUR

There are a series of template styles across this grid, so please choose the most suitable for your needs, while considering the elements and modules that will feature on that template.

 

Do not clutter the templates. Observe information hierarchy to choose the most suitable template and use white space and proximity within it to clearly define the visual hierarchy of on-screen content.
